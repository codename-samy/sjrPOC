About Lufthansa

Deutsche Lufthansa AG is famed as Lufthansa. It is the biggest German and European airline company in terms of the number o passengers travelled and the current fleet size. Its major hubs are based out of Munich and Frankfurt Airport. The various subsidiary airlines of Lufthansa are Swiss International Brussels Airlines, Austrian Airlines and more. The frequent flyer program of the airline is called ''Miles & More'' and it offers outstanding services and amenities to the loyal and consistent customers of the airlines.
Lufthansa was founded in the year 1997 and it has its headquarters based out of Cologne. With a fleet size of about 283 aircraft including Airbus A320-200, Airbus A319-100, Boeing 747-8, Lufthansa has attained a position to be one of the most popular airlines.

Lufthansa Cabins

Lufthansa Airlines Economy Class

The Economy Class of Lufthansa offers comfortable seating configuration, entertainment options, high-speed internet access and distinguished cuisines with a drinks menu.
With the legroom, it also provides you with enough space for soft backrests and armrests which enables you to stretch your legs.
There is a vast array of latest movies, TV shows, documentaries and radio channels which are available for your entertainment.

Lufthansa Premium Economy Class

For a lavish experience without paying much, you can book your seats with Premium Economy Class of Lufthansa. Hence, upgrading to the Premium Economy Class is a good choice.
The seats give you some extra personal space to stretch out with its size which has 50% extra room from each side.
Ample legroom, plush backrests and headrests help your business or holiday trip more enjoyable.
Your flying experience is enhanced by the in-flight entertainment system on a touchscreen TV.
Watch top-rated blockbusters, old classics from the cinema world, most-viewed TV shows or music genres.

Lufthansa Airlines Business Class

Lufthansa's Business Class ensures a relaxed experience. The first-class comfort with Business Class offers including additional baggage allowance, priority boarding and exclusive delicious in-flight meals make your journey memorable.
Newly-designed seats of Business Class can be converted into a fully-flat bed which is apt for relaxation.
The perfect seating configuration with a subtle colour scheme gives a spacious feel.
The entertainment system offers a personal touchscreen TV above the clouds in Business Class.

Lufthansa Airlines First Class

If you are travelling with the First Class of Lufthansa, experience exceptional comfort. You should pamper yourself with the best of the benefits of flying in the First Class of Lufthansa.
The seats of First Class are exceptionally comfortable with plush headrests, armrests and a massage feature that leaves you fresh and energetic.
You also get your own personal assistant to help you out during your journey.
Indulge in the vast world of entertainment with blockbuster movies, latest TV programs, documentaries and games.

Food Facilities Offered by Lufthansa

Lufthansa's main aim is to pamper its flyers to authentic delights with tasty beverages. Sumptuous cuisine is served with fine wines and drinks picked by professional sommeliers.
All the First and Business Class flyers are offered with mouth-watering delicacies who come all the way from Germany.
Premium Economy is known for complimentary meals and drinks that are served on board.
A 3-course sumptuous meal with tasty desserts and healthy salads are served in Economy Class.
When travelling with kids, there is a comprehensive range of healthy culinary offerings for them.
You can order your special cuisines in advance and enjoy them later based on your preferences.
